Privacy Policy for Outsider - Find the Imposter

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction
Welcome to Outsider - Find the Imposter ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you use our mobile application (the "App") and tell you about your privacy rights.

2. Information We Collect
The App is designed to be played locally with friends. We do not require you to create an account, provide a name, email address, or any personal identification to use the core features of the App.

3. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions
We offer premium subscriptions via in-app purchases. If you choose to subscribe, your purchase is processed securely by Apple or Google depending on your platform. We use RevenueCat to manage and verify your subscription status. We may collect and store your purchase history (linked only to an anonymous App User ID) to provide you with access to premium features and allow you to restore purchases across devices. We do not process, collect, or store your payment details or credit card information.

- Google AdMob may use advertising IDs (such as the Android Advertising ID or Apple Identifier for Advertisers) and device data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
- You can learn more about how Google uses your data here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

5. Local Data Storage
The App uses your device's local storage to save your game preferences, custom categories, and match progress. This data remains locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
