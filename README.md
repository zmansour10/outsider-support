# Outsider: Find the Imposter 🎭

**Outsider** is a fun and engaging social deduction party game built with Flutter. Gather your friends, pass the device, and uncover who among you is the **Imposter** who doesn’t know the secret word!

---

## 🎮 About the Game

Outsider is designed for **local multiplayer (pass-and-play)** with 3 or more players. Each round is filled with strategy, bluffing, and laughter as players try to identify who is out of the loop.

* One player is secretly the **Imposter**
* All others know the **Secret Word**
* The Imposter must blend in and guess the word before being caught

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

1. Add at least 3 players
2. Pass the device so each player can see their role privately
3. Players ask **yes/no questions**
4. Discuss and observe suspicious answers
5. Vote for who you think the Impposter is
6. If caught, the Imposter gets one final chance to guess the word
